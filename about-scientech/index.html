About ScienTech

Tuesday, 2 February 2021

ScienTech was created in the idea to have place where I could summarize and share knowledge about science, technology and more. The essence and goal of this blog is to be looked as a toolbox, where you can find apparatus that will help you in your journey and grow your knowledge, in a simple and plain way.

I believe everyone should be learning and understanding the the" why" things work the way they work. For me it is important to figuring out the real porpoise of what we deal with and finally, look to it in a different way and see if it can make it better.
